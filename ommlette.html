Egg Ommlette

What will you need... or Go Back Home

  • 2 Eggs
  • Spices and seasoning
  • (Optional) Veggies

Let's get started...

Step 1: Crack your eggs into a bowl and mix vigourously, add salts or seasoning powder (like garlic powder) if you want to, make sure to add your veggies if you have some

Step 2: Start your stove on a LOW flame and pour your egg "batter" into it... flip until sides are cooked thouroughly

Enjoy!
