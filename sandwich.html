Egg Sandwich

What will you need... or Go Back Home

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Pieces of Bread (preferabbly toasted)
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Mayonaise
  • Hot Sauce
  • (Optional) Veggies

Let's get started...

Step 1: Crack your eggs into a bowl and mix vigourously, add salts or seasoning powder (like garlic powder) if you want to

Step 2: Start your stove on a LOW flame and pour your egg "batter" into it... flip until sides are cooked thouroughly

Step 3: Get your cooked eggs on top of a piece of bread

Step 4: Spread condiments like sauces (mayo and hot sauce are my go-to) on your egg and add your veggies!

Step 5: Put the top bread on and enjoy your sandwich!
